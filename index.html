About Us

We build animated IP by running disciplined, audience-led experiments — without ever compromising character craft or collective artistic integrity.

We partner with animation directors, game studios, and indie creators to deliver feature-quality animation for character-driven stories, in-engine cinematics, and living worlds. We build characters and IP that move across communities and games, staying directly in the hands of your audience.

Animation Folk is a brand new venture in its early stages, led by Joe Dollar‑Smirnov. Joe is a classically trained animator with over 20 years’ experience in tech, designing and making digital work including some of the early wave of web games. Clients include, Space Chicken & Bacon the Cat, Financial Times, Nike, Unilever and more. Alongside that, he’s been part of the startup world: coaching lean, experiment‑driven design and getting involved with early‑stage projects in blockchain and the circular economy.

In this period of experimentation, we are playing with IP development as well as being open to client partnerships.

We bring digital experiences to life through innovative animation techniques, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the Web 3.0 ecosystem.
